About Smoker

Smoker is a biannual event hosted by the Alpha Epsilon of Phi Kappa Sigma Educational Society. It is one of the oldest events hosted at Illinois Institute of Technology campus. All 21+ brothers and alumni are welcome to join our upcoming smoker!

Next Smoker is planned for September 21st, 2019

RSVP for Smoker

This semester, tickets and event details are being handled through Eventbrite. There is also a corresponding Facebook event. If you did not receive an email and Facebook invite, or have any Smoker/Alumni related questions, please contact the Upsilon (upsilon@aepks.com).
