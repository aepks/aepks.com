About Smoker

Smoker is a biannual event hosted by the Alpha Epsilon of Phi Kappa Sigma Educational Society. It is one of the oldest event hosted at Illinois Institute of Technology campus. All brothers 21+ and alumni are welcome to join our upcoming smoker! "This is not your pledge-father's Smoker." You are cordially invited to an all new Fall Smoker. This Smoker we will be switching things up a bit and taking the event off campus. Fall Smoker will be held on Saturday, October 6th, 2018 at the Biggs Mansion (https://biggschicago.com/), a cigar club and lounge in Chicago's Gold Coast neighborhood. We are excited to add some changes while keeping some of our traditions. So please, join us for a Saturday night out in town to catch up with Alumni and Brothers, while you enjoy cigars, drinks and appetizers (catered by Chicago Q https://chicagoqrestaurant.com/) This will be a less formal event than previous Smokers, Attire: Business Casual.
